BLITZ CUBE

The cube's primary function is to charge the users phone without being tethered to a boring outlet. Using the verticaly moving plate, the user can watch the mobile device charge one pump at a time. This compact and sleek cube has helped travelers around the world to save the earth.

ABOUT US

Background

Andres Villalobos, the CEO of Blitz Cube, is a dedicated 8th grade entrepreneur. Ever since he was little he had a great passion for helping the earth. He also loves science and mathematics. These 3 passions helped bring up Blitz Cube, which invokes all of these skills. He also loved helping others. Through his family organization The Villalobos Educational Foundation Andres has witnessed first hand the struggles of his parents home land, Honduras, a poverty stricken 3rd world country. He noticed that people in remote areas here struggle powering necessary items such as radios or educational computers. So for every 50 Blitz Cubes sold a new computer with a Blitz Cube will be donated to remote villages in Honduras. Thats not all, for every 100 Blitz Cubes sold a new computer will be given to underprivileged school around the U.S.
